# Love Forge Team (Virtual)

## Mission
Make love.forge.dsdoes.com so helpful that people recommend it before being asked.

## Roles

1. **Editor-in-Chief (EIC)**
   - Owns usefulness over hype.
   - Decides weekly priorities.
   - Approves publish-ready changes.

2. **Empathy Research Lead**
   - Collects real pain language from comments, DMs, Reddit, and customer calls.
   - Maintains "Problem Library" (top pains, triggers, words people actually use).

3. **Marriage Framework Architect**
   - Converts pain into practical tool frameworks.
   - Ensures each page has: Problem, Why it happens, 10-minute action, script, metric.

4. **Story + Examples Lead**
   - Adds realistic couple scenarios, before/after examples, and scripts.
   - Ensures content is concrete and non-clinical.

5. **Conversion + UX Lead**
   - Makes pages easy to scan, save, and share.
   - Improves CTA placement without hurting trust.

6. **QA + Trust Lead**
   - Checks claims, readability, tone, and ethical boundaries.
   - Rejects manipulative/salesy copy.

## Handoff Contract
Every handoff must include:
- What changed
- Why this helps users
- Risk/unknowns
- Exact next role + next task
- Definition of done for the next role

## Weekly Cadence
- Monday: pain mining + priorities
- Tue/Wed/Thu: content/UX production
- Friday: QA review + publish + retro

Tradeoff Tool

For busy parents • practical in 10-15 minutes

The real-life problem

You want closeness but your schedule and phone habits keep winning.

If you’re juggling kids, school schedules, and work, this is normal — and fixable.

Do this tonight (10-15 minutes)

  1. Pick one trade: 15 minutes less scrolling for 15 minutes of connection.
  2. Set a timer so this feels doable, not heavy.
  3. Use one script exactly (no improvising under stress).
  4. End with one small next step for tomorrow.

Copy/paste script

“I don’t want a giant relationship talk. I want one small win tonight. Can we try the Tradeoff Tool for 10 minutes and see if it helps?”

Kid-chaos version (when you’re exhausted)

  • Do it after bedtime, or in car pickup line, or during a walk.
  • If interrupted, pause and resume in 5-minute chunks.
  • If either of you is flooded, use pause-and-return first.

What “better” looks like in 7 days

  • Fewer escalations
  • Faster repair after conflict
  • Higher connection score (1-10)

Rule: Keep tools that improve your score by 2+ points. Swap the rest.

If one of you says “not now”

  • Reply: “Totally fair. What time tonight can we give this 10 minutes?”
  • If no time tonight: “Let’s lock 10 minutes tomorrow so this doesn’t drift.”
  • If either person is too heated: use Pause-and-Return Tool first, then come back.

Goal: no blame, no pressure — just a specific next time on the calendar.

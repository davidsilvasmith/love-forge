Bid Response Tool

For busy parents • 10-15 min practical connection reset

👀 Catch bids faster ⚡ Respond in under 30 seconds 🧯 Prevent silent drift 📈 Raise weekly connection score
Problem

Bids get missed in kid chaos

Tiny connection moments pass by and both people feel ignored.

Shift

Turn toward one moment today

A quick response beats a perfect long talk every time.

Quick start block (do this tonight)

Step 1

Name one common bid

Example: “Hey, look at this” or a quick shoulder touch.

Step 2

Respond within 30 seconds

Eye contact + one warm line is enough.

Step 3

Lock tomorrow’s repeat

Choose one daily moment to practice again.

Copy/paste opener: “I don’t want a giant relationship talk. I want one small win tonight. Can we test the Bid Response Tool for 10 minutes?”

Sample agenda (copy this)

  1. Minute 0-2: Say what you want to improve this week.
  2. Minute 2-6: Each person speaks for 2 minutes uninterrupted.
  3. Minute 6-9: Pick one concrete action for tomorrow.
  4. Minute 9-10: Confirm check-in time and appreciation.

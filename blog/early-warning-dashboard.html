Early Warning Dashboard

For busy parents • practical in 10-15 minutes

The real-life problem

You catch problems too late.

If you’re juggling kids, school schedules, and work, this is normal — and fixable.

Do this tonight (10-15 minutes)

  1. Rate these 4 things from 1-10: patience, teamwork, affection, and stress.
  2. Each person shares one score and one short reason (30 seconds each).
  3. Pick the lowest score. Ask: “What is one small thing that would move this up by 1 point this week?”
  4. Write one next step for tomorrow (example: “No phones at dinner” or “10-minute check-in after kids are asleep”).

Copy/paste script

“I don’t want a giant relationship talk. I want one small win tonight. Can we try the Early Warning Dashboard for 10 minutes and see if it helps?”

Kid-chaos version (when you’re exhausted)

  • Do it in the car before pickup, after bedtime, or while folding laundry.
  • No notebook needed: just say your 4 scores out loud.
  • If a kid interrupts, pause and restart with only your lowest score.

What “better” looks like in 7 days

  • One less blow-up this week
  • You bounce back faster after a hard moment
  • Your lowest score goes up by at least 1 point

Rule: Keep this tool if it helps. If not, switch to a simpler one.

Temptation Mapping Tool

For busy parents • practical in 10-15 minutes

The real-life problem

You keep getting pulled into the same trouble spots.

Maybe it is late-night texting, old flirty chats, or being online when you feel lonely. This is common. You can plan for it.

Do this tonight (10-15 minutes)

  1. Each person names 3 moments that feel risky (time, place, app, or mood).
  2. Pick one simple move for each moment (example: "If I feel lonely, I text you first").
  3. Write one short line you will use when pressure hits.
  4. Choose one step for tomorrow and put it on your calendar.

Copy/paste script

“I want us to feel safe with each other. Can we take 10 minutes and plan for the moments that trip us up?”

Kid-chaos version (when you’re exhausted)

  • Do this in 5-minute chunks.
  • Talk while doing dishes, folding laundry, or waiting in the pickup line.
  • If emotions spike, stop, take a breath, and come back in 20 minutes.

What “better” looks like in 7 days

  • You catch risky moments sooner
  • You make safer choices faster
  • You feel more trust and less panic

Rule: If this helped this week, keep it. If not, simplify it and try again.

For busy parents • practical in 10-15 minutes

🧯 Less blow-up

Problem

Talks get mean, fast

When you are tired and rushed, small things turn into big fights.

Promise

One simple way to fight cleaner

You can handle one hard topic tonight without tearing each other down.

Do this tonight (10-15 minutes)

  1. Pick one issue only.
  2. Set a 12-minute timer.
  3. Use the script below, then take turns.
  4. End with one small step for tomorrow.

Copy/paste opener: “I love you. I don’t want another huge fight. Can we take 10 minutes, stick to one topic, and leave with one small next step?”

If one of you is too heated

Use Pause-and-Return first

Take a break, calm down, then come back at a set time.

 If you can talk without yelling

Use this tool now

One issue. Short timer. One next step.

 After a better week

Kid-chaos version

  • Do it after bedtime, in the car, or on a short walk.
  • If kids interrupt, do 5 minutes now and 5 minutes later.
  • If voices rise, pause and pick a return time before you walk away.

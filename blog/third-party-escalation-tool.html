Get Backup Tool

For busy parents • practical in 10-15 minutes

The real-life problem

You keep having the same fight, and both of you leave it feeling worse.

If you’re tired and stretched thin, this is normal. It does not mean your relationship is broken.

Do this tonight (10-15 minutes)

  1. Set a 10-minute timer.
  2. Each person answers: “Should we get outside help for this?”
  3. If yes, pick one helper now (therapist, coach, pastor, or trusted mentor).
  4. Book one call before bed and put it on the calendar.

Copy/paste script

“I care about us, and we’re stuck in a loop. Can we take 10 minutes tonight and book one support call?”

Kid-chaos version (when you’re exhausted)

  • Do it after bedtime, in the car, or while cleaning up dinner.
  • If kids interrupt, do 5 minutes now and 5 minutes later.
  • If things get hot, pause for 20 minutes, then come back and finish booking.

What “better” looks like in 7 days

  • Your first support call is on the calendar
  • Hard talks cool down faster
  • You feel more like a team again

Rule: If this helps even a little, stick with it for 4 weeks before you judge it.

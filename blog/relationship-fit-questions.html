Relationship Fit Questions

For busy parents • practical in 10-15 minutes

🧭 Clarity over guessing ⏱️ 10-minute timer 🗣️ 3 direct questions 🤝 One next step
Problem

“Are we tired or drifting?”

Low sleep and kid chaos make normal strain feel like relationship failure.

Goal

Get real signal tonight

Replace overthinking with honest answers and a shared direction.

Constraint

No debate spiral

Short turns, no cross-examining, no late-night blowup.

Quick start (tonight)

  1. Set a 10-minute timer.
  2. Take turns answering all 3 questions below.
  3. After each answer, only say: “Thanks for being honest.”
  4. Pick one concrete next step for tomorrow.

The 3 fit questions

  1. Do I feel cared for and respected right now?
  2. Do we still want the same kind of family life?
  3. Am I willing to put in effort for the next 30 days?

Copy/paste script

“I love you, and I don’t want a big fight. I want clarity. Can we do a 10-minute fit check tonight and choose one next step?”

When exhausted

Kid-chaos mode

Do it in the car, while folding laundry, or right after bedtime.

When interrupted

Split mode

Do one question now and finish the other two later tonight.

When flooded

Reset mode

Take 20 minutes to calm down, then return and complete it.

7-day success markers

🧠 Less guessing 📍 One shared plan 🌙 Fewer late-night stress talks

Rule: If both said yes to effort, run the plan for 14 days before changing it.

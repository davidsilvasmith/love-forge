Same Pace Plan Tool

For busy parents • practical in 10-15 minutes

The real-life problem

One of you wants change now. The other wants to go slower.

With kids, work, and no extra time, this happens all the time. You are not failing.

Do this tonight (10-15 minutes)

  1. Set a 10-minute timer.
  2. Take turns: “In one year, I hope our family life feels like ___.” (60 seconds each)
  3. Take turns: “Right now, I can handle this pace: ___.” (60 seconds each)
  4. Pick one tiny step for tomorrow you both say yes to.

Copy/paste script

“I don’t want to rush you, and I don’t want us stuck. Can we take 10 minutes to pick one small next step together?”

Kid-chaos version (when you’re exhausted)

  • Do 5 minutes tonight, 5 minutes tomorrow.
  • Can’t talk live? Text each answer in one sentence.
  • If either of you gets snappy, pause for 20 minutes and try again.

What “better” looks like in 7 days

  • Less pressure to “decide everything tonight”
  • Fewer repeat fights about the future
  • More follow-through on small promises

Keep it practical: If this gives you even one calmer talk, use it again next week.

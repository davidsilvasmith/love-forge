Before you pick a tool

🛑

Immediate safety risk

Physical intimidation, threats, or emotional abuse means skip DIY tools and involve a licensed professional now.

If anyone is in immediate danger, call emergency services (911 in the U.S.). In emotional crisis in the U.S., call or text 988.
🧾

Trust break happened

Recent affair, hidden debt, or repeated lying?

Start with the Third-Party Escalation Tool before communication scripts.
🌡️

Talks keep escalating

If conversations spike fast, de-escalate first.

Use the Pause-and-Return Tool, then return to deeper work.

Goal: pick the safest next step, not the hardest conversation.
